  • Corners where walls meet ceiling - use painter's tape for clean lines

Project Timeline & Sequencing

Day 1 - Morning (4 hours)

• Surface preparation: patching, sanding, cleaning
• Set up drop cloths and protective coverings
• Tape off trim, windows, and fixtures

Day 1 - Afternoon (4 hours)

• Apply primer to all walls
• Apply primer to ceiling
• Allow 2-4 hours drying time

Day 2 - Morning (4 hours)

• First coat on ceiling
• First coat on walls 1, 2, and 6
• Allow proper drying between sections

Day 2 - Afternoon (4 hours)

• First coat on walls 3, 4, and 5
• Begin trim preparation if time allows
• Inspect for areas needing touch-up

Day 3 - Morning (4 hours)

• Second coat on all walls
• Second coat on ceiling if needed
• Detail work on trim and edges

Day 3 - Afternoon (2-3 hours)

• Remove tape while paint is slightly tacky
• Touch-ups and corrections
• Final cleanup and inspection

Critical Timing Notes: Maintain 65-75°F temperature and 40-50% humidity for optimal drying. Allow minimum 4 hours between coats. Schedule final coat early in day to allow for same-day tape removal.

Quality Standards & Specifications

Surface Preparation Standards

Surface Condition Required Preparation Quality Standard
Nail holes/small cracks Fill with spackling compound, sand smooth No visible imperfections from 3 feet
Previous paint (good condition) Clean with TSP solution, light sanding Smooth, clean surface for adhesion
Glossy surfaces Sand to dull finish, prime No sheen visible, primer adheres well
Water stains Apply stain-blocking primer No bleed-through after two coats

Paint Application Standards

  • Coverage: Uniform color with no shadows or thin spots visible in natural light
  • Finish: Consistent sheen across all surfaces (eggshell for walls, flat for ceiling)
  • Edges: Clean, straight lines at all transitions (wall-to-ceiling, wall-to-trim)
  • Texture: No brush marks, roller marks, or orange peel texture unless specified
  • Coats: Minimum 2 coats on walls, 1-2 on ceiling depending on coverage
Inspection Checklist: Upon completion, inspect all surfaces under both natural and artificial light. Check for holidays (missed spots), drips, sags, or inconsistent texture. Touch up as needed before removing protective coverings.
